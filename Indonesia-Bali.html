Kelingking Beach

Kelingking Beach is a natural gem nestled on the southwestern coast of Nusa Penida Island in Bali.

Suluban Beach

Suluban Beach is a natural gem nestled in the Peninsuula Hill, about 32km from Denpasar.

The Blue Lagoon

The Blue Lagoon is a hidden gem with white sands, azure waters, and vibrant marine life. A tranquil haven for serenity seekers.

Kuta

Ulun Danu Beratan Temple is a picturesque landmark temple in Bali’s central highlands. The temple sits on the western side of Beratan Lake.

Besakih Temple

Besakih Temple is often referred to as Bali’s ‘mother temple’ – a grand complex of different clan temples and shrines on the south-western slopes of Mount Agung.

Tanah Lot

Tanah Lot regularly features on Bali postcards and travel brochures, thanks to its stunning and unique offshore setting.
