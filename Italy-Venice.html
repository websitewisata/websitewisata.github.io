St. Mark Basilica

Standing as a beacon of Venetian grandeur and the city's spiritual heart, St. Mark's Basilica is an architectural masterpiece that has captivated the world for centuries.

Teatro la Fenice

In the heart of Venice, Teatro La Fenice, a renowned opera house for over 200 years, captivates with stunning architecture, impeccable acoustics, and a rich history.

Scuola Grande di San Rocco

Located in the heart of Venice, the Scuola Grande di San Rocco is a magnificent Renaissance building that is home to one of the most important collections of Venetian art in the world.

The Grand Canal

Gliding through the heart of Venice, the Grand Canal is not just a waterway but a vibrant artery, a bustling marketplace, and an enduring symbol of the city's rich history.

Venetian Lagoon

The Venetian Lagoon surrounds the city of Venice and is home to numerous small islands that are worth visiting.

Cannaregio Canal

The Cannaregio Canal runs from the Cannaregio district to the Grand Canal. Its 800-metre length is lined with historical buildings and churches.
